IT
Horror, Drama, Thriller
"It" (2017) is a horror film directed by Andy Muschietti, based on the 1986 novel of the same name by Stephen King. The story revolves around a group of children in the fictional town of Derry, Maine, who are terrorized by a shape-shifting entity that primarily takes the form of a malevolent clown named Pennywise. The entity preys on the children's deepest fears, often manifesting as their worst nightmares. As the children confront their fears and uncover the truth about the entity, they form a bond and make a pact to defeat "It" once and for all. The film explores themes of friendship, bravery, and the loss of innocence, as the children face both the supernatural horrors of Pennywise and the real-world challenges of growing up in a troubled town.

The Grinch
Family, Comedy, Fantasy
"The Grinch" (2018) is an animated Christmas comedy film based on the 1957 Dr. Seuss book "How the Grinch Stole Christmas!" and serves as the third screen adaptation of the story, following the 1966 television special and the 2000 live-action film. The movie tells the story of the Grinch, a cynical and misanthropic creature who lives in solitude atop Mount Crumpit with only his loyal dog, Max, for company. Disliking the festive spirit of the nearby town of Whoville, the Grinch devises a plan to ruin Christmas for the Whos by stealing all of their decorations, presents, and holiday feast. However, his scheme is complicated when he encounters Cindy Lou Who, a young girl with a heart of gold who believes there's more to Christmas than just material possessions.

Infinity War
Superhero, Action, Sci-Fi, Thriller
It is based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers and serves as the sequel to 2012's "The Avengers" and 2015's "Avengers: Age of Ultron." The movie is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The plot revolves around the Avengers and their allies as they come together to stop the powerful and malevolent titan Thanos from obtaining all six Infinity Stones, which would grant him the power to wipe out half of all life in the universe with a snap of his fingers. The film features an ensemble cast, including iconic Marvel characters like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, Spider-Man, Doctor Strange, and many others.

The NUN
Supernatural, Horror
The movie is set in 1952 and follows a priest and a novitiate sent by the Vatican to investigate the mysterious suicide of a nun at a remote Romanian abbey. As they uncover dark secrets and confront malevolent forces, they encounter a demonic entity in the form of a nun, which terrorizes them and puts their faith to the ultimate test. "The Nun" explores themes of faith, evil, and the supernatural, delivering chilling scares and intense moments of suspense. With its eerie atmosphere, religious imagery, and terrifying apparitions, "The Nun" is a spine-tingling addition to the horror genre, offering fans of "The Conjuring" series another chilling installment.
